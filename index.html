About

We aim to raise obesity awareness amongst university students. Obesity is a very serious health issue and should be recognised due to the risks involved with living an unhealthy lifestyle.

To achieve this we will provide many health related articles and information, diet and fitness plans. Our goal is to promote being healthy and active, as well as provide tips and advice!

Privacy Policy

FTF is strongly committed to protecting your privacy when you interact with us, our content, products and services. Our goal is to provide you and your family with media experiences that inform, entertain and educate. Sometimes this means that we use information that you provide to us about yourself to customise that experience. We do this to improve your enjoyment of our products and services. In providing our services to you, the FTF will be transparent about how and why we collect and use your information. In some cases, if you do not want us to collect or use your information in a particular way, then we will give you the opportunity to say so. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to explain: the application of this Privacy Policy the kind of information the FTF may collect about you, how we collect it, and how we use it how we may disclose that information how you can access the information we hold about you when we may use your information to contact you the protection of your personal information the FTF's use of cookies to collect information, and how you can control or delete these cookies additional steps to protect young people.
